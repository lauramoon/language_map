Most Common Languages in Illinois

Background

Excluding English and Spanish, Polish is the most common language spoken at home in Illinois. But when Illinois is broken up into smaller regions, dozens of other languages show up as the most common across the state after excluding English and Spanish.

The regions on this map are from the U.S. Census Bureau's Public Use Microdata Areas. Each region has at least 100,000 residents.

The language data is from the Census Bureau's 2018 American Community Survey, which collapses over 1300 languages and language groups into about 130 languages and language groups for its Public Use Microdata Samples.

Colors used for map and legend from https://carto.com/carto-colors/.
Cartographic boundary files from the Census Bureau converted to geoJson format by https://mygeodata.cloud/
